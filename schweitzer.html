Schweitzer — winter cabin memories

Since I was eight, my family and I have returned every winter to the cabin near Schweitzer. The warmth of the fire, the crisp mountain air, and the steep runs welcomed me home again and again.

Each stay is small and rich: boots drying by the hearth, the smell of wood smoke in the morning, and that hush after a fresh snowfall when the world feels new. Time spent there with my family is one of my longest-held, favorite memories — a quiet tradition I carry with me always.
